About

About us

Digital design and software services for museums, galleries, archives and other cultural institutions

Our client's needs are as varied as their surroundings, but the method tends to be consistent. Take a look at our typical project process

Lvl.1 Discovery

Initiation

An initial briefing to provide you with questions relating to your goals and ambitions, including any specific requirements

Lvl.1 Discovery

Project plan

We establish the scheduling, teams and key components of the project to fit with you

Lvl.1 Discovery

User research

Your unique expectations and requirements direct our research and approach (including what to avoid), allowing this to inform us on decision making

Lvl.2 User Experience

Information Architecture

The first major step in the design process is to produce a site map to rationalise the core structure of a products navigation and templates

Lvl.2 User Experience

User journey

Building user-journeys and personas, we map the user-flow and digital connectivity

Lvl.2 User Experience

Wireframes

We design the complete structure of the product, including how it behaves and functions

Lvl.2 User Experience

UX workshop

At this stage we run a workshop to discuss our vision for the functionality of the product. This is an opportunity to critique and ensure the product fits your needs

Lvl.3 User Interface

Branding

Analysis and building a digital identity from your existing brand, or developing a completely new visual system if required

Lvl.3 User Interface

Art direction

We begin drafting the ideation for the visual communication of the product, the digital treatment and tone of its graphic expression

Lvl.3 User Interface

UI workshop

A collaborative workshop to review the visual direction, examining the graphic and interactive styling

Lvl.3 User Interface

Interface design

With your feedback, we construct the user-interface design for the complete digital product and further iterate it with you

Lvl.4 Development

Design guide

Once the product has been approved, we create a design manual to explain the form and function of the interface to our development team

Lvl.4 Development

Data mapping

Our developers begin by integrating and configuring your database with our Qi CMS

Lvl.4 Development

Development

We plan and develop the full product as an alpha version; ready to be user-tested internally

Lvl.4 Development

User testing

We provide formal usability testing to ensure quality control and consistency across platforms

Lvl.5 Launch

Software training

If needed, we offer training and guides for managing your content and collection with our popular CMS software Qi

Lvl.5 Launch

Launch product

Your product goes live and is released to the public when you're ready. We tend to plan a release date in advance and deliver to the required date

Lvl.5 Launch

Maintenance & support

Once your product goes live, we offer a range of supporting services to ensure it remains in good working order post-release
